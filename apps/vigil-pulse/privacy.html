Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

1. Data Ownership & Local Processing

Vigil Pulse is designed with a privacy-first architecture. All deterministic state management and market data visualization occur strictly on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, financial identifiers, or API keys to any external servers.

2. No Data Collection

The application does not require a user account and does not utilize cloud synchronization. We do not have access to your market views, workspace configurations, or any data you input into the application. All data remains on your local hardware.

3. No Tracking or Analytics

Vigil Pulse does not use third-party analytics, tracking cookies, or advertising identifiers. Your usage of the application remains entirely anonymous and private.

4. Data Deletion

All application data is stored locally on your device. You have full control over this data. You can remove all information at any time by deleting the application or clearing its local storage through the iOS/iPadOS Settings app.
